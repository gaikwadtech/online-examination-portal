## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of the TestEdge portal seriously. If you discover a vulnerability in our platform, please report it to us immediately and confidentially.

**Please DO NOT open a public GitHub issue.**

### How to Report

Please send a detailed email to our security team at:

**gaikwadpratik964@gmail.com**

In your report, please include:
* A description of the vulnerability.
* Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
* The affected version(s) of the code.
* Any necessary proof-of-concept (POC) code or screenshots.

### Our Commitment

We commit to:
1.  Acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours.
2.  Provide an estimated time frame for addressing the vulnerability.
3.  Maintain open communication throughout the process.
4.  Publicly credit you for the discovery (if you agree).
